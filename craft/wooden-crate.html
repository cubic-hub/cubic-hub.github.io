Wooden Crate

A basic storage block used in many builds.

Components

  • Wood Plank × 8
  • Nails × 4

Station

  • Basic Workbench

Steps

  1. Arrange 4 planks to form the crate frame.
  2. Attach remaining planks to form sides, top, and bottom.
  3. Reinforce corners with nails.

Notes

Simple recipe; fast to craft at low-level stations.
